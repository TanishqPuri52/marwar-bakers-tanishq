Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we offer delivery within a 10-mile radius of our store. Delivery fees apply based on distance.

How far in advance should I place a cake order?

We recommend placing cake orders at least 48 hours in advance. For custom or specialty cakes, please allow 3-5 days.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes, we offer gluten-free, vegan, and nut-free options. Please specify your dietary requirements when placing an order.

Do you offer catering services?

Yes, we provide catering services for events of all sizes. Please contact us directly to discuss your specific requirements.
